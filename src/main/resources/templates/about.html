About BatCare

Your help in need

BatCare is design to save the day for bats who save the day! With the graphic masterpiece "BatList", no task is too small for the caped crusader to handle.

As Alfred, the Rolls Royce of admins, registers the tasks and organize them in their right subject columns, Batman can spend his time on execution.

No more trying to remember what the next move is, just pick up the BatPhone and take on the list. So Batman can focus on what he does best: kick some villains ass!
